Jean's Club

Recommended for Time Management Game fans.

♥ No.1 time management game!!

Jean start new business, managing club after the big hits of Jean's Boutique Fashion.

Serve customers as fast as you can to keep them happy.
If the customers wait for too long, they get angry and eventually leave the club.
Clean! Dance! Music!

Challenge to the best club through upgrade and employee.

▣ Gameplay Concept
▷ The more customer's satisfaction is high, the more tip you earn.
▷ If the customers wait for too long, they get angry and eventually leave the club.
▷ You will get random rewards to clear the stage with higher score.
▷ You will get many rewards to beat a challenge.
▷ The employee help you to run a club.
▷ Use items and upgrade for getting the higher rating.
▷ Do not forget to clean the dance stages.

▣ Features
▷ 10 clubs with 10 levels to beat in each club.
▷ Various useful items to help you with running the club.
▷ Inviting friends and sharing the game reward you in various ways.
▷ Opening a new club the game reward you in various ways.
